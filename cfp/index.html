Call for Papers

The Workshop on Cyber-Securing Democratic Elections elicits two kinds of submissions:

  • Images of Democracy (A2 Poster)
  • Posters presenting research on democracy and cyber-security

Submission Information

Images of Democracy: we invite participants to use visual imagery and a simple short description to tell people about their democracy-related research and its benefits. The image could be a photograph, a drawing or digital creation or an image from research directly. It should highlight why democracy research is happening and why it makes a difference. Please submit a PDF no bigger than A2

Democracy and Cybersecurity Poster Format: An A2 sized poster. Use this template.

Submission Link: https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/DRW2023

Submission deadline: May 30, 2023
Notification to authors: June 7, 2023

Attending participants must register by April 30, 2023 (Early Registration)
Late Registration permitted until 15 May, 2023.

Manuscripts will be reviewed by the program chairs.

Manuscripts SHOULD NOT be anonymous.

For general questions about the workshop, please contact Anthony McGann (anthony.mcgann@strath.ac.uk).
